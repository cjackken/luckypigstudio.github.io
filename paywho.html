How It Works

3 Steps. Done.

1
Add Participants

Enter everyone's names — the people who were part of the outing, regardless of what they shared.

2
Add the Bills

For each bill: enter the amount, who paid, and who's splitting it. Different bills can have different people.

3
PayWho

Tap Split Bill. PayWho calculates the simplest set of transactions — exactly who pays who, and how much.

Why PayWho

Built for Real Situations

Multiple bills, multiple payers

Alice paid for dinner, Ben got the cake, Claire ordered drinks. PayWho handles it all in one go.

Custom participants per bill

David skipped the cake? No problem — just uncheck him from that bill. Each bill has its own participant list.

Instant results

No spreadsheets. No mental arithmetic. Hit Split Bill and the answer is right there — who pays who, down to the cent.

No login. No account.

Open the app, split the bill, close the app. Nothing stored in the cloud. Nothing to sign up for.

Completely free

No in-app purchases. No premium tier. PayWho is free to use, always.

Works offline

No internet connection needed. Use it at the restaurant, at the chalet, anywhere — it just works.
