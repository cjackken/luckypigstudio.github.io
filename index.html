Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Lucky Pig Studio ("we", "us", or "our") operates the BaoTio mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

Information We Collect

BaoTio is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Device Advertising ID — Used by our ad partners (Google AdMob, IronSource/LevelPlay) to serve relevant advertisements.
  • App usage data — Anonymised crash reports and performance data to improve app stability.
  • Lucky number history — Stored locally on your device only. We do not collect or store your lucky numbers on our servers.

Advertising

BaoTio is free to use and supported by advertising. We use the following ad networks:

  • Google AdMob
  • IronSource / Unity LevelPlay

These partners may collect data as described in their respective privacy policies. You can opt out of personalised advertising through your device settings.

Data Storage

Your lucky number history and app preferences are stored locally on your device using Android/iOS local storage (PlayerPrefs). This data is not transmitted to our servers and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Our API

BaoTio connects to our secure API to retrieve your daily unique lucky number. This API call does not transmit any personally identifiable information — only an anonymous request is made to claim a number from the daily pool.

Children's Privacy

BaoTio is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: support@luckypigstudio.com
