Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Lucky Pig Studio ("we", "us", or "our") operates the BaoTio and HIT 1000 mobile applications. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our apps.

Information We Collect

Our apps are designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Device Advertising ID — Used by our ad partners (Google AdMob, IronSource/LevelPlay, Liftoff Monetize, Unity Ads) to serve relevant advertisements.
  • App usage data — Anonymised crash reports and performance data to improve app stability.
  • Game/app data — Stored locally on your device only. We do not collect or store your lucky numbers or game scores on our servers.

Advertising

Our apps are free and supported by advertising. We use the following ad networks: Google AdMob, IronSource / Unity LevelPlay, Liftoff Monetize (Vungle), Unity Ads. These partners may collect data as described in their respective privacy policies. On iOS, we request App Tracking Transparency permission before serving personalised ads.

Data Storage

Your lucky number history, app preferences, and game scores are stored locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to our servers and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

BaoTio API

BaoTio connects to our secure API to retrieve your daily unique lucky number. This API call does not transmit any personally identifiable information — only an anonymous request is made to claim a number from the daily pool.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
