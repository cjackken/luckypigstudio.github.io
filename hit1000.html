The Concept

Simple yet addictive. A number runs at high speed—your job is to stop it as close to the target as possible. Hit the target, and the challenge escalates!

Dynamic Difficulty

Success moves the goalposts. Target 1000 becomes 2000, then 3000 and so on. As the numbers climb, the speed intensifies, demanding split-second precision.

Privacy Policy

Lucky Pig Studio ("we", "us", or "our") operates the HIT 1000 mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

Information Collection and Use

HIT 1000 is designed to be a local, high-score based reflex game. We do not require you to provide any personally identifiable information to use the app.

  • Game Data: Your high scores and progress are stored locally on your device. We do not store this data on our servers.
  • Log Data: In the event of an error, we may collect basic technical information (device type, OS version) through third-party services to help us fix bugs.
